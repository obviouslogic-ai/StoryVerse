# Independent Audit Plan Policy

## Purpose
Define SSC’s approach to independent audit planning and assessment cadence.

## Scope
All SSC systems and services in scope for independent assessments.

## Compliance Status
SSC is not currently certified under SOC 2 or HIPAA. This policy defines readiness controls.

## Policy
- Plan independent assessments based on risk and compliance requirements.
- Define scope, frequency, and assessment standards.
- Maintain independence from control owners during assessment activities.

## Standards and Procedures
- Maintain an annual assessment plan and scope.
- Document auditor qualification and independence criteria.
- Record assessment outcomes and required remediation.

## Roles and Responsibilities
- Compliance Lead: maintains audit plan and selects auditors.
- Executive Sponsor: approves assessment scope.
- Control Owners: provide evidence and remediate findings.

## Review and Exceptions
- Review annually or upon significant changes.
- Exceptions require documented approval.
