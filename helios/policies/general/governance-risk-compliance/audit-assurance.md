# Audit and Assurance Policy

## Purpose
Define SSC requirements for audit readiness, internal control assurance, and independent assessments.

## Scope
All SSC systems, processes, and personnel supporting the SSC Cloud product and corporate IT.

## Compliance Status
SSC is not currently certified under SOC 2 or HIPAA. This policy defines readiness controls.

## Policy
- Maintain documented audit and assurance procedures aligned to applicable standards.
- Plan audits based on risk, regulatory requirements, and stakeholder expectations.
- Track audit findings and corrective actions to closure.

## Standards and Procedures
- Maintain an annual audit plan and scope statement.
- Document audit roles, responsibilities, and evidence collection procedures.
- Maintain a remediation plan for findings with owners and due dates.
- Regularly review and report remediation status of audit findings to relevant stakeholders.
- Review audit procedures annually or upon significant change.

## Roles and Responsibilities
- Executive Sponsor: approves audit scope and remediation priorities.
- Compliance Lead: maintains audit procedures and coordinates assessments.
- Control Owners: provide evidence and remediate findings.

## Review and Exceptions
- Review annually or upon significant changes.
- Exceptions require documented risk acceptance.
