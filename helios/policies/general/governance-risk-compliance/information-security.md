# Information Security Policy

## Purpose
Establish baseline security governance to protect confidentiality, integrity, and availability of information assets and services.

## Scope
All SSC employees, contractors, systems, environments, and third parties that access or process SSC data, including the SSC Cloud product.

## Compliance Status
SSC is not currently certified under SOC 2 or HIPAA. This policy defines SSC's target controls and is maintained as part of our compliance readiness program.

## Policy
- Maintain a documented security program that defines objectives, controls, roles, and responsibilities.
- Apply defense-in-depth controls across identity, data, application, infrastructure, and monitoring layers.
- Require secure configuration baselines for systems and services.
- Ensure security controls align with regulatory obligations, customer contracts, and risk tolerance.
- Align security program with the NIST SP 800-53 control framework as the foundational security baseline.
- Apply ITIL-based process governance for service management, incident management, and change management.

## Standards and Procedures
- Maintain a security charter and control catalog aligned with NIST SP 800-53 controls.
- Define and enforce secure configuration baselines for production and non-production.
- Perform periodic security control reviews and remediation tracking.
- Maintain a centralized security exceptions process with approvals and expiry dates.
- Conduct monthly security control reviews for core infrastructure and applications.
- Maintain compliance readiness against SOC 2, HIPAA, and NIST SP 800-53 frameworks.

## Roles and Responsibilities
- Executive Sponsor: approves the security program and risk appetite.
- Security Lead: owns policy maintenance and control oversight.
- Engineering: implements and operates technical controls.
- All Personnel: adhere to security requirements and training.

## Review and Exceptions
- Review annually or upon significant change.
- Document exceptions with risk assessment, approval, and expiration.
