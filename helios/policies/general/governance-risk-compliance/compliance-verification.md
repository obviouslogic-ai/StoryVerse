# Compliance Verification Policy

## Purpose
Define how SSC identifies and verifies compliance with applicable standards, laws, and contractual obligations.

## Scope
All SSC systems, services, and business processes supporting the SSC Cloud product.

## Compliance Status
SSC is not currently certified under SOC 2 or HIPAA. This policy defines readiness controls.

## Policy
- Identify applicable regulatory, legal, and contractual requirements.
- Verify compliance through periodic reviews and internal controls.
- Maintain documented evidence of compliance activities and outcomes.

## Standards and Procedures
- Maintain a compliance requirements register.
- Perform annual compliance reviews and document outcomes.
- Track compliance gaps with owners and remediation plans.

## Roles and Responsibilities
- Compliance Lead: maintains the requirements register and review schedule.
- Legal: validates regulatory and contractual requirements.
- Control Owners: implement and evidence compliance controls.

## Review and Exceptions
- Review annually or upon significant changes.
- Exceptions require documented approval and remediation plan.
