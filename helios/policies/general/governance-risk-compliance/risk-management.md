# Risk Management Policy

## Purpose
Identify, assess, and manage risks to information systems and regulated data.

## Scope
All SSC business processes, systems, and third parties supporting the SSC Cloud product and corporate IT.

## Compliance Status
SSC is not currently certified under SOC 2 or HIPAA. This policy defines SSC's target controls and is maintained as part of our compliance readiness program.

## Policy
- Maintain a formal risk management program with documented risk assessments.
- Evaluate risks for confidentiality, integrity, availability, and regulatory compliance.
- Track risks with owners, mitigation plans, and due dates.
- Integrate risk outcomes into security, product, and operational planning.

## Standards and Procedures
- Maintain a risk register with impact, likelihood, and treatment strategy.
- Conduct risk assessments at least annually and upon major changes.
- Review third-party risks prior to onboarding and periodically thereafter.
- Escalate material risks to executive stakeholders.

## Roles and Responsibilities
- Risk Owner: accountable for mitigation actions.
- Security Lead: facilitates assessments and maintains register.
- Executive Sponsor: approves risk acceptance decisions.

## Review and Exceptions
- Review annually or upon significant changes.
- Risk acceptance must be documented with rationale and expiry.
