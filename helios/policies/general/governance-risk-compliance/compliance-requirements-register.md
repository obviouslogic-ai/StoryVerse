# Compliance Requirements Register Policy

## Purpose
Define how SSC identifies, records, and maintains compliance obligations.

## Scope
All SSC systems and services, including the SSC Cloud product.

## Compliance Status
SSC is not currently certified under SOC 2 or HIPAA. This policy defines readiness controls.

## Policy
- Maintain a centralized register of regulatory, legal, and contractual requirements.
- Review and update the register at least annually.
- Communicate changes to relevant stakeholders.

## Standards and Procedures
- Record requirement source, scope, owner, and review cadence.
- Track requirement changes and impact assessments.
- Align the register with risk and audit planning.

## Roles and Responsibilities
- Compliance Lead: owns the requirements register.
- Legal: validates legal and contractual requirements.
- Control Owners: implement requirements within their domains.

## Review and Exceptions
- Review annually or upon significant changes.
