# Stakeholder Engagement Policy

## Purpose
Define how SSC engages with relevant industry groups and external stakeholders for compliance and security awareness.

## Scope
All SSC compliance and security-related outreach activities.

## Compliance Status
SSC is not currently certified under SOC 2 or HIPAA. This policy defines readiness controls.

## Policy
- Maintain contact with relevant external stakeholders when applicable.
- Track engagements and knowledge exchanges that inform SSC controls.
- Use stakeholder input to inform risk and compliance planning.

## Standards and Procedures
- Maintain a list of relevant industry and regulatory contacts.
- Document engagement outcomes and applicable actions.
- Review engagement priorities annually.

## Roles and Responsibilities
- Compliance Lead: manages stakeholder engagement activities.
- Executive Sponsor: approves participation in external initiatives.

## Review and Exceptions
- Review annually or upon material changes.
