# Environment Risk Classification Policy

## Purpose
Define SSC requirements for classifying environments based on risk and data sensitivity.

## Scope
All SSC environments including production, staging, development, and testing.

## Compliance Status
SSC is not currently certified under SOC 2 or HIPAA. This policy defines readiness controls.

## Policy
- Classify environments by data sensitivity and threat exposure.
- Apply security controls appropriate to the classification.
- Review classifications annually or upon significant change.

## Standards and Procedures
- Maintain an environment classification register.
- Document controls required per environment classification.
- Reassess classifications after major architecture changes.

## Roles and Responsibilities
- Security Lead: defines classification criteria.
- Engineering: implements controls per classification.

## Review and Exceptions
- Review annually or upon significant changes.
- Exceptions require documented approval.
