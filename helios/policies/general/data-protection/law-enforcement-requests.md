# Law Enforcement and Legal Requests Policy

## Purpose
Define SSC procedures for receiving and responding to law enforcement or legal requests for data.

## Scope
All SSC systems and data subject to legal or law enforcement requests.

## Compliance Status
SSC is not currently certified under SOC 2 or HIPAA. This policy defines readiness controls.

## Policy
- Require legal review of all law enforcement requests.
- Validate request authority and scope before disclosure.
- Maintain an auditable record of requests and responses.

## Standards and Procedures
- Maintain a standard intake and verification process for requests.
- Notify affected parties where legally permitted.
- Document disclosures and retention of records.

## Roles and Responsibilities
- Legal/Compliance: owns request validation and response.
- Security Lead: supports data identification and access controls.

## Review and Exceptions
- Review annually or upon significant changes.
- Exceptions require documented approval.
