# Data Flow Documentation Policy

## Purpose
Define SSC requirements for documenting data flows across systems and services.

## Scope
All SSC systems that create, process, store, or transmit customer and internal data.

## Compliance Status
SSC is not currently certified under SOC 2 or HIPAA. This policy defines readiness controls.

## Policy
- Maintain data flow documentation for key systems and data categories.
- Review data flow documentation at least annually or upon major changes.
- Ensure data flows capture data sources, destinations, and transfer mechanisms.

## Standards and Procedures
- Maintain a data flow diagram inventory.
- Update diagrams after major architecture or integration changes.
- Record data transfer methods and encryption controls.

## Roles and Responsibilities
- Data Owners: validate data flow documentation accuracy.
- Engineering: updates diagrams and technical flow details.

## Review and Exceptions
- Review annually or upon significant changes.
- Exceptions require documented approval.
