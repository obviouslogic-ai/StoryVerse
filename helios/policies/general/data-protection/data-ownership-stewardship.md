# Data Ownership and Stewardship Policy

## Purpose
Define SSC requirements for assigning data ownership and stewardship.

## Scope
All data processed, stored, or transmitted by SSC, including the SSC Cloud product.

## Compliance Status
SSC is not currently certified under SOC 2 or HIPAA. This policy defines readiness controls.

## Policy
- Assign data owners for each data class and system.
- Define data steward responsibilities for quality and protection.
- Review ownership assignments at least annually.

## Standards and Procedures
- Maintain a data ownership register.
- Document owner responsibilities and approval authority.
- Update assignments after organizational or system changes.

## Roles and Responsibilities
- Data Owners: accountable for data classification and access approvals.
- Data Stewards: ensure data quality and compliance with handling rules.

## Review and Exceptions
- Review annually or upon significant changes.
- Exceptions require documented approval.
