# Privacy Impact Assessment Policy

## Purpose
Define SSC requirements for performing privacy impact assessments (PIA/DPIA).

## Scope
All SSC systems or changes that involve personal data or ePHI.

## Compliance Status
SSC is not currently certified under SOC 2 or HIPAA. This policy defines readiness controls.

## Policy
- Perform a PIA/DPIA for new systems, major changes, or new data uses.
- Document privacy risks and mitigation actions.
- Review and update assessments upon material change.

## Standards and Procedures
- Maintain a PIA/DPIA template and assessment register.
- Include data flows, data categories, and legal bases.
- Track mitigation actions to closure.

## Roles and Responsibilities
- Privacy Officer: owns the PIA/DPIA process and approvals.
- Security Lead: reviews security risk implications.
- Product Owners: initiate assessments for changes.

## Review and Exceptions
- Review annually or upon significant changes.
- Exceptions require documented approval.
