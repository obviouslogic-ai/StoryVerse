# Privileged Access Governance Policy

## Purpose
Define SSC requirements for granting, limiting, and reviewing privileged access.

## Scope
All privileged roles and administrative access to SSC systems.

## Compliance Status
SSC is not currently certified under SOC 2 or HIPAA. This policy defines readiness controls.

## Policy
- Grant privileged access only when required and for a limited period.
- Prevent accumulation of privileged access across roles.
- Require documented approval for high-risk privileged access.
- Conduct periodic privileged access reviews.

## Standards and Procedures
- Maintain privileged access request and approval procedures.
- Review privileged access at least quarterly.
- Remove or downgrade privileged access when no longer required.

## Roles and Responsibilities
- Security Lead: owns privileged access governance.
- System Owners: approve and review privileged access.

## Review and Exceptions
- Review annually or upon significant changes.
- Exceptions require documented approval.
