# Threat Analysis Policy

## Purpose
Define SSC requirements for threat analysis, threat modeling, and threat landscape assessment.

## Scope
All SSC Cloud systems, services, and infrastructure.

## Compliance Status
SSC is not currently certified under SOC 2 or HIPAA. This policy defines readiness controls.

## Policy
- Perform threat analysis to identify, assess, and review the threat landscape.
- Build threat models according to industry best practices to inform risk mitigation.
- Reassess threats upon significant changes or emerging risks.
- Integrate threat analysis outcomes into vulnerability management and risk planning.
- Use a risk-based method for the prioritization and mitigation of threats, leveraging an industry-recognized framework to guide threat decision-making and protection measures.

## Standards and Procedures
- Define threat analysis methodology and cadence.
- Document threat models and risk mitigation strategies.
- Review threat landscape at least annually or upon significant change.
- Align threat analysis with vulnerability detection and response processes.

## Roles and Responsibilities
- Security Lead: owns threat analysis process and methodology.
- Engineering: provides technical inputs and implements mitigations.

## Review and Exceptions
- Review annually or upon significant changes.
- Exceptions require documented approval.
