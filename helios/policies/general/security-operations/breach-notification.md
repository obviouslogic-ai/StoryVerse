# Breach Notification Policy

## Purpose
Define requirements for notifying stakeholders and regulators of security or privacy breaches.

## Scope
All incidents involving unauthorized access, use, or disclosure of regulated data.

## Policy
- Evaluate incidents for breach notification obligations.
- Notify regulators without undue delay, and in any event within 48 hours of becoming aware of a reportable breach.
- Escalate critical breach notifications internally within 1 hour of confirmation.
- Provide a written breach report within 24 hours of the initial escalation.
- Coordinate communications through legal and compliance; external communications require executive approval.
- Maintain breach records and corrective actions.

## Standards and Procedures
- Classify incidents and determine notification triggers.
- Maintain breach notification templates and contact lists.
- Document all notifications and communications.
- Engage qualified forensic investigators to determine root cause, data types involved, and scope of impact.
- Develop a communication plan addressing internal employees, the public, and directly affected parties.
- Perform post-breach reviews and update controls.
- Produce a final breach report including: extent of breach, data compromised, evidence and logs, corrective actions, and security impact assessment.

## Roles and Responsibilities
- Legal/Compliance: determines notification obligations.
- Incident Commander: coordinates response and reporting.
- Security Lead: conducts investigation and root cause analysis.

## Review and Exceptions
- Review annually or after a material breach.
- Exceptions require documented legal approval.
