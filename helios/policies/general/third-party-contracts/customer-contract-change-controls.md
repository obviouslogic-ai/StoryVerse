# Customer Contract Change Controls Policy

## Purpose
Define SSC requirements for changes that impact customer environments, contractual obligations, and service agreements.

## Scope
All changes that affect SSC customer environments, SLAs, and contractual commitments.

## Compliance Status
SSC is not currently certified under SOC 2 or HIPAA. This policy defines readiness controls.

## Policy
- Require explicit authorization for changes impacting customer environments.
- Ensure contractual obligations are reviewed prior to changes.
- Document customer notifications for material changes.
- Include provisions in service agreements specifying data access upon contract termination, including data format, storage duration, scope of retained data, and data deletion policy.
- Ensure service agreements incorporate mutually agreed provisions covering: scope and location of services, information security requirements, change management process, logging and monitoring capability, incident management and communication procedures, right to audit and third-party assessment, service termination, interoperability and portability requirements, data privacy, and operational resilience.

## Standards and Procedures
- Maintain a change impact assessment for customer-affecting changes.
- Review contract and SLA commitments prior to release.
- Track customer communications for material changes.
- Ensure service agreements address data portability and termination provisions.
- Review service agreement completeness against required provisions at least annually.

## Roles and Responsibilities
- Customer Success: manages customer communication for changes.
- Legal/Compliance: reviews contractual obligations and service agreement provisions.
- Engineering: implements changes per approved process.

## Review and Exceptions
- Review annually or upon significant changes.
- Exceptions require documented approval.
