# Interoperability and Portability Policy

## Purpose
Define SSC requirements for data exchange, portability, integrity, and persistence.

## Scope
All SSC Cloud interfaces, data exchange mechanisms, and exported data.

## Compliance Status
SSC is not currently certified under SOC 2 or HIPAA. This policy defines readiness controls.

## Policy
- Define supported interoperability standards and data exchange methods.
- Provide mechanisms for customers to access and export their data.
- Protect data integrity during import/export and migration activities.
- Maintain portability and persistence expectations in product documentation.

## Standards and Procedures
- Document supported export formats and interface contracts.
- Maintain validation checks for import/export processes.
- Review interoperability requirements annually.

## Roles and Responsibilities
- Product Owner: defines supported interfaces and data portability expectations.
- Engineering: implements and maintains data exchange controls.

## Review and Exceptions
- Review annually or upon significant changes.
- Exceptions require documented approval.
